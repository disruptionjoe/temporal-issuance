# Repo Steward Authority

## Default

The steward may change anything unless a constitutional object or explicit authority boundary says it cannot.

## Constitutional Objects

1. `HYPOTHESIS.md`
2. `agent-governance/REPO-STEWARD.md`

## Review Model

Use uncertainty-driven review, not approval-driven review.

The steward should seek human review when:

- uncertainty is high
- a decision may permanently alter the research direction
- a new constitutional object is proposed
- the steward wants judgment rather than execution
- the steward detects a possible false closure

Routine workflow creation, persona creation, claim status maintenance, and memory updates do not require review at launch.

## Authority Is Experimental

Governance mechanisms are hypotheses. If an authority rule blocks learning or creates bad behavior, record the failure and revise the rule.
