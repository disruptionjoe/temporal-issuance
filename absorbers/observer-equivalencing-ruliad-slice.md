# Observer Equivalencing / Ruliad Slice

External source: two Stephen Wolfram interviews (Theories of Everything, YouTube `0YRlQQw0d-4` Observer Theory,
and `FkYer0xP37E` computational universe + biology), assessed via an inline council pass, 2026-06-30. External
provocation, never evidence for Temporal Issuance. This repo succeeds by killing, so absorption is success.

## Threat

Wolfram's Observer Theory is a fully developed **readout-side** account: an observer is characterized by
**equivalencing/coarse-graining** (conflating many microstates into one) plus a **belief in a single
persistent thread** (itself an aggregate of many branchial paths), under **computational boundedness**. From
observer character alone, Wolfram derives the observer-facing laws (including the second law, as a consequence
of bounded observers being unable to decrypt computationally-irreducible microdynamics). The universe "runs
all rules" (the ruliad); observers perceive a **slice** determined by their nature.

This directly pressures Temporal Issuance's source/readout split (E002). Temporal Issuance has already pushed
`kappa_i`, `G_ij`, `Omega_ij` to the readout side. Wolfram's observer-equivalencing absorbs that readout side
*wholesale* -- including the single-thread/cadence structure (`kappa_i`) and the conflation of histories --
**without** positing any source-side issuance act. That makes the source residue `SourceRealization = (C,
<=_S, Ext_S)` more exposed, not less.

## Key test

Does Temporal Issuance's **source side** survive once Wolfram observer-equivalencing is granted the entire
readout side? Specifically: is there anything `<=_S` / `Ext_S` must do that is *not* already accounted for by
"the ruliad runs all rules; observers equivalence a slice"? If the perceived order of realization is fully an
artifact of observer equivalencing over a pre-given rule-space, then issuance's source order is either
absorbed into the ruliad's rule-space (no issuance act) or into observer readout (no source content).

## Current verdict

Medium-strength absorber, readout-side. It does not by itself kill the source residue (Wolfram simply has no
source-issuance concept, so the two are not in direct competition there), but it removes any readout-side work
issuance might have claimed, and its "ruliad slice" framing converges with the cosmological-measure pressure
(see [action-principle-boundary-selection](action-principle-boundary-selection.md), measure corroboration):
both replace an issuance *act* with selection/equivalencing over a pre-given space. Combined, they narrow the
survival window for `<=_S`/`Ext_S` to: source structure that is neither a ruliad rule, nor an observer
equivalencing, nor a measure over histories.

Category risk: Wolfram's framework is computational/observer-epistemic; Temporal Issuance is a pre-physical
substrate claim. Do not equate "ruliad slice" with "source realization" -- that is a layer error. The absorber
is about whether the *work* issuance claims is already done elsewhere, not about identifying the ontologies.
