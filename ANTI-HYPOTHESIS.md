# Anti-Hypothesis

The Temporal Issuance hypothesis may be false, absorbed, confused, or non-research-grade.

Plausible failure modes:

1. It is only causal set theory with different language.
2. It is only block-universe structure described procedurally.
3. It is only the thermodynamic arrow renamed.
4. It is only cosmological expansion renamed.
5. It is only information growth or record formation renamed.
6. It smuggles ordinary time into `kappa_i`, `dR`, or the realization order.
7. Its issuance measure `mu` collapses into entropy, action, volume, information, energy, or probability.
8. Its frontier `dR` is metaphysical decoration with no formal work to do.
9. Its observer-indexed cadence cannot be defined without assuming the thing being explained.
10. It produces no nontrivial theorem, discriminator, model, or test.

Absorption is not automatically a stop signal. Every absorption claim must ask:

- Is this actually absorbed, or are we comparing the wrong categories?
- Is this actually novel, or have we simply renamed something?
- Is this failure local to one formulation, or does it kill the core?
