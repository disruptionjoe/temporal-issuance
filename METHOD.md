# Method

## Core Method

1. Generate the strongest surviving version of the hypothesis.
2. Attack that version from first principles.
3. Map existing frameworks that may absorb it.
4. Preserve provenance.
5. Record what died, survived, weakened, clarified, promoted, or should be exported.

## Research Over Governance

Governance exists to serve the research verdict.

During launch, governance may consume substantial attention. Long term, the steward should shift toward research unless governance failures are blocking the research mission.

## Mathematics

Approach mathematics from first principles.

Use existing mathematics when appropriate, but do not treat existing mathematics as automatically authoritative. Check category alignment and framing alignment.

The repo may create proofs, derive structures, and invent new objects when necessary. New structures must be justified, not merely proposed.

## Absorption Discipline

Absorption claims must include:

- absorber framework
- mapping
- what is explained
- what is not explained
- category-error risk
- local-minimum risk
- resurrection trigger
