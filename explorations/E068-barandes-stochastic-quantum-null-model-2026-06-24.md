## TI Use Rule

For any TI-C020-like physical-source proposal, ask first:

```text
can the claimed transition already be modeled as a stochastic process whose
effective behavior is absorbed by CPTP evolution plus unitary dilation on a
fixed Hilbert space?
```

If yes, the proposal has not yet earned H-growing or A-growing status.

## Why This Matters

Some candidate proposals look novel because they change:

- accessibility,
- prediction quality,
- subsystem partition,
- coarse-graining,
- measurement context,
- stochastic presentation.

But if those changes fit inside a fixed-H / fixed-channel absorber, they are
still reinterpretations of a fixed ambient structure rather than evidence of
source-side issuance.

## Current Verdict For TI

Barandes strengthens the null side:

```text
stochastic novelty is not enough;
fixed-H absorbers now include a broader stochastic-to-unitary completion class.
```

This is therefore a hostile filter for TI, especially in quantum-facing or
predictive-accessibility arguments.

## What Would Escape The Absorber

A TI proposal still needs to show something like:

1. observable algebra genuinely grows,
2. admissible state space genuinely grows,
3. no fixed ambient Hilbert space with access maps reproduces the transition
   while preserving the same operational typing,
4. the witness survives perturbation, basis change, and subsystem redescription.

Without that, Barandes should be treated as an absorber.
