# E099: Rendered 4D Interface - 63-Persona Triple Steelman

## Purpose

Joe clarified the target after E098:

```text
The 14D GU-like geometry is not merely an outside record structure casting a 4D shadow.
It may be the larger quantum/geometric arena we inhabit. Evolved observers are driven by
that geometry, biology, and action constraints to render a shared 4D Standard-Model-facing
interface. Quantum mechanics is where we still touch more of the larger structure. GU's
missing boundary/firewall/adapter piece may connect the 14D geometry to an external stable
record structure outside the full GU manifold and outside the rendered 4D shadow.
```

1. **Novel steelman** - the most original research idea this persona sees.

3. **Publishable steelman** - the version that could survive peer-facing discipline first.

```text

```

```text
record-geometry makes stable and salient enough to become a shared interface object.
```

```text
Do not say the 14D structure passively casts a 4D shadow.
```

```text
missing piece has Standard-Model-like character.
```
