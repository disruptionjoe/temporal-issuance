# E192: Turbulent Cascade Candidate v0

## Question

Does a turbulent energy cascade supply a physical source-side issuance witness?

The candidate is serious because a driven flow can develop coherent vortices,
intermittent bursts, inertial-range transfer, and dissipation structure not
visible in a smooth initial state. If the source-side story were right, this
would need to be more than ordinary evolution inside a predeclared fluid state
space.

## Frozen Fixed Rival

The rival is fixed before evaluation:

- Navier-Stokes or Euler governing equations;
- fluid density and viscosity;
- domain geometry and boundary conditions;
- initial velocity and vorticity field;
- forcing and energy-injection schedule;
- Reynolds-number regime and scale window;
- subgrid or closure family;
- stochastic perturbation tape;
- measurement protocol and completed flow history.

This rival is allowed to preserve velocity snapshots, vorticity, pressure,
energy spectra, structure functions, dissipation rate, forcing records, and
boundary measurements.

## Result

The candidate is killed as `CANDIDATE_KILLED_FIXED_NAVIER_STOKES_CASCADE`.

The physical signal is real, but the source object is missing. Vortex
stretching, inertial-range transfer, intermittency, and coherent structures are
represented as the nonlinear evolution of a fixed fluid law under fixed
parameters, forcing, boundary data, stochastic perturbations, and measurement
access. The successor context is an evolved state of the same predeclared
phase space, not a source-owned expansion of admissible dynamics.

CompletionClass v1 returns `PHYSICAL_PREDICTIVE_ABSORPTION`.

## Exact Missing Object

The resurrection burden is a source-owned cascade rule generation law: a
native physical source law in which turbulence creates a new eddy-mode algebra,
admissibility predicate, transfer operator, or construction space not
representable as fixed fluid equations, fixed fluid parameters, fixed forcing,
fixed boundary and initial conditions, fixed Reynolds regime, fixed closure
family, stochastic perturbation, observer access, or completed history.

It would also need an internal anti-after-naming principle plus W4 perturbation
non-factorization under the same preserved velocity, vorticity, pressure,
spectrum, structure-function, dissipation, forcing, and boundary records.
