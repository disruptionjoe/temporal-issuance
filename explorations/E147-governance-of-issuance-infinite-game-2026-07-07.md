# E147: Governance of Issuance as an Infinite Game (intake + hypothesis)

Records a multi-part argument developed by Joe in chat, 2026-07-07, building a ladder from the physics

## The argument (Joe's, restated)

1. **Why govern capability?** Containment. A bounded region lets its occupants evolve freely without
   endangering the exterior — the interior's free evolution is quarantined behind the boundary. (This
   is the firewall / bounded-region move already in the repos.)
2. **Why issuance?** To admit new possibility space (genuinely new admissible structure), the
   `TI` source question.
3. **Why govern the issuance?** Anti-capture. New possibility must not all flow to one actor
   (corruption / collusion). The governance that prevents this is a **consistent rate of issuance,
   broadly distributed, then synthesized through the system** — rather than concentrated, unsynthesized
   distribution to a few participants.
4. **Consistency → legitimacy.** Consistency of a monetary policy confers legitimacy; more generally,
   consistency of a *resource* policy confers legitimacy. A consistent, well-defined issuance rate is
   what makes the governance accepted rather than captured.
5. **Legitimacy → infinite game.** A consistently-governed, broadly-distributed issuance keeps the
   system playing an *infinite* game (continued play) rather than a *finite* game that collapses.
   Broad (vs narrow) issuance maximizes the diversity of evolutionary mutations / possible adaptations,
   which is how the system finds new emergent possibility spaces and fights off collapse.

## The physics reframe this forces (the falsifiable core)

"Broadly distributed, consistent rate, synthesized rather than concentrated" is, on the cosmological
side, the **cosmological principle (homogeneity + isotropy) + a governed expansion rate + structure
formation.** A governance *violation* would look like concentrated or wildly-varying issuance dumped on
a few regions without synthesis — which is not what is observed. So the governance hypothesis has an
observable shape: the issuance (dark-energy) history should be consistent (well-defined rate), broadly
distributed (homogeneous), and synthesized (condenses into structure). This connects to the standing
`absorbers/cosmological-expansion.md` and to Weinstein's dark-energy-as-responsive-FIELD reading
(which fits a governed, responsive budget better than a fixed constant) recorded in the GU cross-exam.
