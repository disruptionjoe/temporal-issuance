## What Was Learned

Assembly Theory still carries a real formal/local insight:

```text
source-generated constructor provenance can witness W2/W3:
new admissibility and construction-space growth relative to a prefix.
```

But current empirical Assembly protocols do not add the missing physical controls:

```text
W1 non-isomorphic observable/instrument algebra growth
W4 perturbation non-factorization
W6 absorber defeat against fixed chemistry/search/access/schema
```

The physical problem is not record preservation. W5 is easy: empirical protocols preserve logs,
spectra, annotations, lineages, and product records. The hard problem is that the records remain
explainable inside fixed chemistry, fixed search, fixed stochastic exploration, bounded access,
instrument schema, or fixed observable algebra.

supplies:

```text

    stochastic discovery, access widening, or modeler-added schema

```

Until then:

```text

Assembly Theory != current physical bridge
```
