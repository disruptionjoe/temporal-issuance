# E123: Everett Fixed-Unitary-Source Absorber Gauntlet

## Purpose

Execute E122 Gauntlet B:

```text
Under the repo's effect typing, is Many-Worlds branching Issue[S] or
Project[O] - and either way, what follows for the hypothesis?
```

This is an absorber gauntlet, not a promotion pass. It tests whether Everett
branching supplies source issuance or instead makes the fixed-source /
external-completion absorber concrete in physical dress.

## Typed Elements

| Everett element | Effect typing | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Universal wavefunction plus fixed unitary dynamics | fixed-source generator / absorber input, not `Issue[S]` | The whole branch structure is generated by one predeclared evolution law over the state. No new source-side admissibility predicate, construction space, or generator is formed at the branch event. |
| Decoherence-selected branching | `Project[O]` | Branching is a record/access structure relative to decohering subsystems and coarse-grained observables. It changes what branch-local observers can stably distinguish, not the source generator. |
| Branch-relative records | `Finalize[R]` | Records become branch-stable and mutually reinforcing inside a decohered branch. This is finality/reconstruction vocabulary, matching the repo's prior Quantum Darwinism absorber. |
| Lost/recoverable interference for branch-local agents | `Lose[K]` | Interference terms become practically inaccessible or coarse-grained away for the observer. This is a loss-kernel / projection residue, not source formation. |
| Born weights / branch measures | probability/readout module, not `Issue[S]` | Whether supplied by trace rule, decision theory, envariance, typicality, or another module, the weight rule assigns measure over already generated branch structure. |

## Adversarial Branch: Self-Location As Issue[S]

Strongest attempt:

```text
Before observation, a branch-local agent has self-locating uncertainty over
which future branch-copy she will be. When the record is observed, a new
indexical fact becomes actual for that agent. Could that be Issue[S]?
```

Result: not under the current effect discipline.

The new fact is branch-relative indexical information. It updates the
observer's location inside an already generated branch structure. That is
`Project[O] + Finalize[R] + Lose[K]`: projection to a branch-relative record,
stabilization of that record, and loss of access to incompatible interference
or sibling-branch records. It does not form a new admissibility predicate, a new
source-side construction rule, or a new non-isomorphic observable algebra.

The stronger "Born-rule self-location is source issuance" route would need one
of the following:

1. A branch event that changes the source generator itself, not just an
   observer's branch index.
2. A rule by which branch-local record formation creates a source-side
   admissibility predicate unavailable to the universal dynamics.
3. A W1/W4-W6 physical adapter witness: non-isomorphic observable algebra,
   perturbation non-factorization, record preservation, and absorber defeat.

No such witness appears in the Everett typing. The adversarial branch is
therefore absorbed.

## Fixed-Point Contact With E121

E121 proves, inside the Lean total Nat-indexed family model, that absorption
never closes: after extending a fixed family to absorb a diagonal, the extended
family has its own escaping diagonal.

That theorem does **not** automatically refute the Everett absorber. Everett's
physical source is presented as a single fixed generator: universal state plus
unitary dynamics. It is not automatically a stage-extended family of the kind
modeled by E121's `extendFamily`. Treating the universal wavefunction as an
internally indexed family for a bounded observer is precisely the D-FORK in
quantum dress, not a settled consequence.

What E121 can honestly contribute here is a warning about quantifier order:

```text
Per-stage or per-observer diagonal escape does not by itself defeat a fixed
larger source that precontains the branch space.
```

Everett is the clean physical version of that warning.

## What Would Reverse This Verdict

The result should be reopened if a future candidate supplies a physical adapter
where a branch event produces source-side structure not precontained by the
universal dynamics:

- non-isomorphic observable algebra or construction-space growth;
- a new admissibility predicate formed by the physical process itself;
- perturbation non-factorization that cannot be absorbed into initial state,
  Hamiltonian, boundary condition, measurement interaction, or observer
  coarse-graining;
- preserved records strong enough to distinguish source issuance from
  branch-local projection/finality.

Absent that, Everett remains an absorber, not an issuance witness.
