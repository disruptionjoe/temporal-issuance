## Hegelian Analysis

### Thesis

```text
Adapter_P is now strong enough: if a physical candidate maps into Gamma_n, Adm_n, e_n, w_n,
Gamma_{n+1}, and tau_n, and passes W1-W6, then the candidate can be treated as a possible
physical instantiation of OnlineIssuance^LC.
```

### Antithesis

```text
Adapter_P is still mostly a formal sieve. It does not yet specify the invariants, protocols,
proof objects, physical observables, or model-comparison methods required to tell source growth
from fixed-H, fixed-A, fixed-boundary, access, salience, gauge, or experimenter-added schema.
```

### Synthesis

```text
Adapter_P is valid as a gate, but incomplete as a scouting instrument. The missing layer is a
candidate-scoring rubric that turns W1-W6 into operational subtests: algebraic invariant,
admissibility predicate, construction provenance, perturbation non-factorization, record
preservation, and gauge/access/fixed-source absorber checks.
```

## Meta-Synthesis

The personas converge on seven missing pieces:

1. **Invariant layer**:
   W1 needs a real invariant, not a vibe. Candidate scouting must ask what algebraic,
   topological, geometric, representation-theoretic, index-theoretic, or resource-theoretic
   object changes.

2. **Witness/proof layer**:
   W2/W3 need witness objects. A candidate must say what counts as `w_n` and why it is produced
   by the physical source rather than by a theorist, instrument, observer, or model update.

3. **Null-model layer**:
   The fixed-source nulls must be operational, not just listed. Each candidate should be scored
   against fixed-H, fixed-A, fixed-instrument, fixed-stochastic/collapse, fixed-boundary,
   bounded-access, and experimenter-added-schema absorbers.

4. **Record/provenance layer**:
   `tau_n` needs support structure: records, provenance DAG, finality/confidence condition, and
   what makes the trace shared enough to audit.

5. **Intervention/perturbation layer**:
   W4 and W5 require actual protocols. Candidate scouting must ask what perturbation would fail
   to factor through fixed dynamics while preserving the same records.

6. **Gauge/access/language layer**:
   W6 is under-specified. Candidate scouting must control for relabeling, context change,
   access expansion, level-of-detail change, salience, and observer-render language.

7. **Stop-rule layer**:
   If no candidate can state a concrete W1-W6 obligation, TI-C020 should remain parked and the
   route should switch to `online_issuance_core_verdict_bundle` or proof-assistant hardening.
