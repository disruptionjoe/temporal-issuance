## Existing frameworks that already absorb much of the viewpoint

First, ŌĆ£PA-O2-faithfulŌĆØ is **fidelity to the repoŌĆÖs own fixture contract**, not a formalization of Peano Arithmetic, true arithmetic, or even Robinson arithmetic.
