## Branch 3: Holonomy Route

### Minimal Formal Construction

Let `ExtCat_hol` be a category or groupoid with at least one nontrivial loop. Minimal example:

```text
one object S
endomorphism group generated by r: S -> S
r != id_S
```

Let `G` be a structure group and define a connection/parallel-transport functor:

```text
A: ExtCat_hol -> BG
```

where `BG` is the one-object category whose endomorphisms are `G`.

The holonomy of a loop `gamma` is:

```text
Hol_A(gamma) = A(gamma) in G
```

If:

```text
A(r) != 1_G
```

then holonomy is nontrivial.

This proves a formal conditional:

```text
If ExtCat has nontrivial loops and a non-flat connection, then Ext_S has a morphism-level
holonomy invariant not recoverable from the induced preorder.
```
