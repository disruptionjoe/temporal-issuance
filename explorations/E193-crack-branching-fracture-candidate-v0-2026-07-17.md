# E193: Crack Branching Fracture Candidate v0

## Question

Does dynamic crack branching supply a physical source-side issuance witness?

The candidate is serious because a loaded body can form new crack branches,
new free surfaces, branching topology, acoustic emission bursts, and rough
fracture morphology that were not present as macroscopic surfaces in the
initial specimen. If the source-side story were right, this would need to be
more than ordinary damage evolution inside a predeclared material and fracture
state space.

## Frozen Fixed Rival

The rival is fixed before evaluation:

- elastodynamic or phase-field fracture law;
- material microstructure and flaw distribution;
- specimen geometry and boundary conditions;
- loading history and loading rate;
- initial crack state;
- toughness, energy-release criterion, process-zone model, and dissipation
  law;
- stochastic disorder tape;
- measurement protocol and completed fracture history.

This rival is allowed to preserve crack-tip trajectories, branch maps,
fracture surfaces, stress and strain fields, load-displacement traces, acoustic
emissions, high-speed images, and boundary/load records.

## Result

The candidate is killed as `CANDIDATE_KILLED_FIXED_FRACTURE_MECHANICS`.

The physical signal is real, but the source object is missing. Crack branching,
new free-surface formation, acoustic bursts, and fracture morphology are
represented as evolution under a fixed fracture law with fixed material
microstructure, defects, geometry, loading, boundary conditions, fracture
criterion, stochastic disorder, measurement access, and completed fracture
history. The successor context is an evolved damage state in the same
predeclared mechanics, not a source-owned expansion of admissible dynamics.

CompletionClass v1 returns `PHYSICAL_PREDICTIVE_ABSORPTION`.

## Exact Missing Object

The resurrection burden is a source-owned fracture-interface generation law: a
native physical source law in which fracture creates a new interface algebra,
admissibility predicate, branch-selection operator, or construction space not
representable as fixed elastodynamics, fixed material microstructure, fixed
flaws, fixed geometry, fixed loading, fixed boundary conditions, fixed fracture
criterion, stochastic disorder, observer access, or completed history.

It would also need an internal anti-after-naming principle plus W4 perturbation
non-factorization under the same preserved crack-tip, branch,
fracture-surface, stress, strain, load, acoustic-emission, image, and boundary
records.
