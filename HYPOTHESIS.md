# Hypothesis

## Core Hypothesis

Reality is a shared, distributed, observer-participatory process that remains open-ended
because of ongoing issuance: the continual introduction of new possibility, capacity, or
opportunity into the shared process.

Observers hold local state, store records, learn, self-reference, pursue goals, and build
models. But observers are not isolated. Their updates cohere because they participate in a
common underlying process. That process is not closed. Issuance is what keeps it from
collapsing into a closed rearrangement of prior state, and is therefore what keeps reality
capable of producing genuinely new structure.

## Center Of Gravity

The organizing question for this repository is:

> Why does reality remain capable of producing genuinely new structure?

It is **not**, in the first instance:

> Why does time flow?

Temporal order and finality are downstream observer-side reconstructions from records. They
are real targets of explanation, but they are not the deepest claim, and the project should
not try to win by deriving the arrow of time directly from records or finality.

## Long-Range North Star

The long-range ambition remains physical:

> Issuance may ultimately be connected to what physics describes as energy, cosmological
> expansion, structure formation, and the continuing capacity of the universe to support
> observers, novelty, and evolution.

This bridge has **not** been earned. It is the central research target, not an established
result. See `NORTH-STAR.md` for the full layered statement, misreadings to avoid, and the
research burden that makes issuance precise.

## Stronger Mathematical Guess

Reality may be modeled as a shared participatory process whose state is not a closed update
of prior state, because an issuance term continually extends the space of admissible
possibility. Observer histories are lossy, locally-cadenced projections of that shared,
issuance-extended process.

## Plain English Version

Many observers share one world because they take part in one underlying process. That process
keeps getting new room added to it — new possibility, not just rearrangement of what already
exists. Without that ongoing addition, the world would eventually become a closed computation
that only reshuffles its past. Issuance is the source-side opening that keeps novelty, agency,
learning, and evolution alive. What we experience as time is how observers reconstruct order
from their records of this process.

## Relationship To The Prior Formulation

The prior constitutional formulation was:

> The deepest global substrate may not be matter, energy, information, spacetime, or records,
> but temporal issuance: the monotone realization of constraint from earlier to later states,
> modeled as a growing measured realization order with observer-indexed record cadences.

That formulation is retained as a downstream, observer-side reconstruction layer, tracked by
claim `TI-C001` and its descendants. The adversarial absorber program (relativity,
thermodynamics, information theory, causal-set growth, measurement, and reconstruction/gluing)
showed that the realization-order framing is too easily absorbed when used as the deepest
claim. The clarified center of gravity moves the deepest claim to shared participation plus
issuance, and demotes monotone realization order to the reconstruction layer where it
survives.
