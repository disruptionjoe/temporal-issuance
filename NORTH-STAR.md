# North Star

This document holds the clarified North Star for Temporal Issuance. It accompanies the
constitutional core hypothesis in `HYPOTHESIS.md` and is the canonical place for the layered
statement, the misreadings to avoid, and the research burden.

## Clarified North Star

The strongest current formulation is:

> Reality is a shared, distributed, observer-participatory process. Observers hold local state,
> store records, learn, self-reference, pursue goals, and build models. But observers are not
> isolated. Their updates cohere because they participate in a common underlying process. That
> process is not closed: it includes ongoing issuance, meaning the continual introduction of new
> possibility, capacity, or opportunity into the system. This issuance keeps reality open-ended
> rather than allowing it to collapse into a closed rearrangement of prior state.

The center of gravity moves from "Why does time flow?" to "Why does reality remain capable of
producing genuinely new structure?"

This does not abandon the energy/cosmology ambition. It clarifies the missing intermediate
layer: between observers reconstructing time from records and the physics of energy and
expansion, there is a source-side opening of possibility that the prior framing left implicit.

The long-range North Star remains physical:

> Issuance may ultimately be connected to what physics describes as energy, cosmological
> expansion, structure formation, and the continuing capacity of the universe to support
> observers, novelty, and evolution.

That bridge has not yet been earned. It is the central research target, not an established
result.

## Core Hypothesis vNext

The clarified structure is layered:

```text
Shared participatory process
    |
    v
Ongoing issuance
    |
    v
Energy / cosmology / expansion / structure formation
    |
    v
Observers
    |
    v
Records
    |
    v
Temporal reconstruction
```

What changed, and what it is not, follows from this ordering. The prior framing could too
easily be read as: finality creates time; records cause temporal flow; finality derives the
thermodynamic arrow; issuance is just another name for physical energy; issuance is just
causal or event growth; or there is a hidden universal clock. None of those are the intended
claims.

The absorber pressure was useful precisely because it showed the project should not try to win
by deriving the arrow of time directly from records or finality. That path is too vulnerable to
existing frameworks: causality, thermodynamics, information theory, causal-set growth,
measurement, and reconstruction/gluing. The stronger project is not mainly about temporal
direction. It is about open-ended reality.

### Why Issuance Matters

The key insight is that a universe made only of observers updating existing state risks
becoming a closed computation.

Without issuance, observers only rearrange what already exists; novelty eventually runs down;
the future becomes increasingly determined by inherited state; adaptive search loses genuine
openness; and agency becomes bounded by a fixed possibility space.

With issuance, new possibility enters the shared process; observers are not the sole source of
what reality becomes; adaptive search remains alive; evolution can continue producing new
strategies; agency has room to discover and create; and shared reality avoids collapse into a
closed loop of prior state.

This is why issuance is not just "change." It is the source-side opening of possibility.

## Layer Separation

These layers must be kept explicit and not collapsed into one another.

### 1. Observer records

Observers store records, compare records, stabilize witness support, and reconstruct temporal
order. This is the observer-side layer. Temporal order and finality should remain downstream
reconstructions from records unless stronger arguments are earned. This is where the prior
realization-order formulation (`TI-C001` and descendants) lives.

### 2. Shared participation

Observers are not independent solipsistic state machines. They participate in a shared process
that lets many local updates cohere into one world. The "shared compute" analogy can be useful,
but avoid literal simulation-theory language. The point is coordinated distributed
participation, not external hardware.

### 3. Issuance

Issuance is the ongoing introduction of new possibility into the shared process. It should not
yet be reduced to entropy, energy, event growth, expansion, or information flow. The intended
research program is to test whether issuance can eventually connect to those physical concepts
without being absorbed by them.

### 4. Physical correspondence

The conjectured connection from issuance to energy, cosmological expansion, and structure
formation. This is the long-range target. It is not an established result and must be earned
through the research burden below.

## Misreadings To Avoid

This is **not** finality-first physics. Finality is not currently claimed to create time.

This is **not** a thermodynamic-arrow derivation. Issuance has not yet derived entropy,
irreversibility, or the physical arrow of time.

This is **not** causal-set growth by renaming. If issuance is modeled as event growth, it must
explain what it adds beyond existing causal growth frameworks.

This is **not** simulation theory. "Shared process" does not mean the universe is literally
running on external hardware.

This is **not** a hidden universal clock. The shared process may be common, but it is not
necessarily time in the ordinary coordinate sense.

This is **not** an already-earned energy theory. The issuance-energy-cosmology bridge is the
intended North Star target, not a result currently established by the repo.

## Research Burden

The next work should focus on making issuance precise. Open questions to drive that work:

1. What is the minimal definition of issuance?
2. What does a closed observer-update system fail to explain?
3. What distinguishes issuance from ordinary stochastic update?
4. What distinguishes issuance from information flow?
5. What distinguishes issuance from causal/event growth?
6. What distinguishes issuance from thermodynamic entropy?
7. What would count as evidence that issuance is not merely a relabeling of known physics?
8. How could issuance eventually connect to energy, expansion, and structure formation?
9. What toy model can compare: closed observer update; stochastic update; external input;
   genuine issuance; and observer-side temporal reconstruction?

A successful precision pass should yield a minimal definition of issuance plus at least one
discriminator that separates it from stochastic update, information flow, causal/event growth,
and thermodynamic entropy. Absent such a discriminator, issuance should be treated as a
relabeling candidate and pressured accordingly.

## Status Of The Energy/Cosmology Connection

The issuance-energy-cosmology connection is an intended target of the program. It is not an
optional side speculation, and it is not an earned result. It defines the long-range direction
of the work while remaining a claim under active adversarial pressure (`TI-C019`). The prior
direct-physics routes (Poincare energy-momentum, BMS soft charges, holonomy) were closed or
left as conditional formal residue precisely because they tried to reach physics without the
issuance layer; the clarified program inserts that layer as the thing to make precise first.

## One-Sentence Summary

Temporal Issuance should be reframed as the hypothesis that shared observer reality requires
ongoing issuance into a common participatory process, and that this issuance may be the missing
source-side concept needed to eventually connect novelty, agency, evolution, energy,
cosmological expansion, and coherent world-formation.
